Solak - Ecology & Solar Energy WordPress Theme.

== Description ==

Solak is a WordPress theme designed for Ecology & Solar Energy. It features a flexible header, custom colors, custom menu, custom logo, and more. The theme is designed to be accessible, translation-ready, and fully responsive.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `solak` folder to your `/wp-content/themes/` directory.
2. Activate the theme through the ‘Themes’ menu in WordPress.
